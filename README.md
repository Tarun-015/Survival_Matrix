# Survival_Matrix

The Indian Business Genome – Series Overview

The Indian Business Genome is a data-driven research series that decodes the patterns, psychology, and survival strategies behind Indian companies and consumers.
Each project in this series explores a different layer of India’s business DNA — from language and behavior to market endurance and consumer intelligence.
Together, they form a comprehensive study of how data, technology, and strategy shape India’s evolving enterprise landscape.


Survival Matrix (ML) — “Decoding What Makes Companies Thrive”

Part of The Indian Business Genome series.
A machine learning study on what drives the survival, success, or shutdown of Indian startups.
By analyzing funding, sector, team, and market data, this project builds predictive models and data-driven insights to understand the DNA of thriving businesses in India’s startup landscape.
